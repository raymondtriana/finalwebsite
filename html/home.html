The importance of free speech

In our modern world there is an increasing reliance on the power of the internet. Messaging boards and forums are no longer tight knit communities of a few hundred. Most large businesses offering a platform for speech hold such power over communication that the question of where a public square begins, and private company ends is a constant dilemma. From President Trump’s controversial de-platforming to outright genocide being facilitated through the internet, control over who speaks is an endless drive for governments worldwide. I intend to shine a light upon the tactics of multiple actors including governments and companies around the world to promote a culture of censorship and the tools available to those that want to speak freely and anonymously. After all anonymity is a right to us all and the ability to share our ideas shouldn’t be governed by nameless faceless entities behind closed doors.
